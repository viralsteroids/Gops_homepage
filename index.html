Does GridOps replace Excel?

No. Excel stays the source model. GridOps helps convert it into an explicit, governed model build you can deploy and update.

Planning models with clear structure ‚Äî time series, drivers, assumptions blocks, rollups, scenarios. Send us one and we'll tell you within a day what converts cleanly and what needs adjustment.
